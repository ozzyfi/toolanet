Get critical technical work right.
Know what works.

ToolA shows technicians the relevant procedure, equipment history and past technical cases during work; it captures field evidence and makes technical deviations visible.

When a fault occurs, it records the diagnostic process, ruled-out causes and root cause; it turns the intervention and verified outcome into your organization’s technical memory.

AFTER THE WORK

An intervention should not end with a record that simply says "Fault resolved." or "Part replaced."

Learn what works.

ToolA structures the technical case.

TECHNICAL CASE STRUCTURE
Symptom → Evidence → Ruled-out causes → Root cause → Intervention → Part / material → Verified outcome
OUTCOME CAN BE VERIFIED OVER TIME
  • —Did the fault occur again?
  • —Did measurements and test values return to normal?
  • —Did the replaced part actually solve the problem?
  • —Did the same intervention work in similar cases?

The team can therefore see not only what was done, but what actually worked.

Information created during installation becomes part of the equipment’s technical history. If a fault occurs at the same location later, the team can review the original installation information, materials used, measurements and test results together with previous maintenance and fault records. The root cause of the fault and the outcome of the intervention are added to the same technical history.

ToolA does not replace final engineering approval. It helps ensure that the required evidence is captured and that defined technical deviations are visible.
